Jay Lichty

Ukulele Craftsman

Photography by Corrie Woods
Owner of Lichty Guitars

In a small shop tucked into the foothills of the Blue Ridge Mountains, Jay Lichty builds custom handmade acoustic guitars and ukuleles. When the Great Recession crippled the construction business in 2008, Jay turned his woodworking experience and craftsmanship from building homes to building guitars and ukuleles. After making his first one and taking a 10-day building workshop, he was hooked and hasn’t looked back.

 

Jay has crafted over 200 instruments in the past seven years, each piece one-of-a-kind and heirloom quality that will last for generations. His ukuleles and guitars have been purchased all over the globe, to hobbyists as well as professionals like Bonnie Raitt.

Ziggy

I love dogs. I have always had one in my life. We got Ziggy from the local animal shelter. I was home eating lunch when I saw her picture in the paper as the “pet of the week”. I put my sandwich down and showed the picture to my wife. We jumped in the car and raced over to meet her. I know she is only on loan from the Universe and that makes her even that much more awesome.
